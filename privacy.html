Privacy policy

Snazzy Paws Grooming respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Information we collect

We may collect personal information from you, including your name, contact details, location, interactions with us, and billing or purchase information.

How we use your information

We collect this information so that we can contact customers about bookings, process payments for services, and better understand our customers’ needs.

Providing information

Providing personal information is optional. If you choose not to provide contact details, we may be unable to book grooming services in advance or set up a returning customer account. If you choose not to provide payment details, we will be unable to process payments through our website.

How we store and protect your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe by storing it securely and limiting access to authorised personnel only.

How long we keep your information

We keep personal information for up to two years after your last visit. After this period, the information is securely deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and to ask for it to be corrected if you believe it is incorrect.

If you would like to request a copy of your information or ask for it to be corrected, please contact us at info@snazzypaws.co.nz or by phone on +64 00 000 0000.
